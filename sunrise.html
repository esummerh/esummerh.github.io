Sunrise

Sunrise is a mobile iOS application designed to support individuals impacted by violence or abuse. Created with discretion and empathy at its core, the app provides users access to emergency contacts, secure journaling features, support forums, and local resources - all within a calming, inconspicuous interface. Developed as part of a capstone project, Sunrise merges technical implementation with trauma-informed design to ensure safety, privacy, and usability for those seeking help.
