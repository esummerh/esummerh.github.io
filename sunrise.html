Capstone Project - Sunrise

Sunrise is a trauma-informed mobile application built to empower individuals navigating experiences of violence or abuse. Designed with privacy, discretion, and emotional safety in mind, the app offers tools like secure journaling, peer support forums, emergency contacts, and resource directories - all wrapped in a calming, non-intrusive interface. Developed during a senior capstone semester at the University of Michigan, Sunrise blends compassionate design with technical rigor to provide a supportive digital space for those seeking help and healing. The app was created by a team of five interdisciplinary students, combining skills in software engineering, user experience design, and trauma-informed research. Over the course of the semester, we collaborated using tools like Figma, Jira, GitHub, and Xcode to plan, prototype, and implement the app. Each feature was carefully designed and tested with an emphasis on accessibility, anonymity, and user empowerment.
